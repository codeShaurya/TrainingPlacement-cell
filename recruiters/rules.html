Rules & Procedures

The procedure for effective campus recruitment is as follows

The Training & Placement Cell invites Companies to visit IIT (ISM) and conduct the process to offer Full Time/Internship to the students. The T&P Cell provides the link to download the Job Notification Form (JNF) & Internship Notification Form (INF). The JNF and/or INF needs to be filled in by the company. The completed form (s) is to be sent, through Email (tp@iit.ism.ac.in),to the T&P Cell.

The companies which did not receive an invitation from the T&P Cell can also follow the above mentioned procedure for submission of JNF and/or INF.

On receipt of JNF/INF, the T&P Cell will allow the company to visit the campus for a pre-placement talk (PPT) either before the selection process or during the final recruitment process.

The T&P Cell finalizes a suitable date for the selection process and communicated to the company. Dates are allotted on the basis of the students perception of the job/internship offered, viz

1. Job profile and Growth prospects

2. CTC/Stipend Offered by Company

3. Past record of recruitment at IIT (ISM)

After completion of the selection process, the company is requested to furnish the final list of selected and wait-listed students.

Facilities at the Placement Office

The placement office is well equipped and is designed to smoothly handle and support the placement procedure at every stage. Arrangements for pre-placement talks, group discussions and interviews are handled by the staff at the placement office. We are equipped with the following facilities to aid the process:

Well furnished, air-conditioned rooms for interviews and group discussions.

Fully computerized office with Wi-Fi Connectivity.

Auditoriums close to the office to conduct pre-placement talks and tests.

Video conferencing facility for conducting Interviews.

Student placement volunteers to assist on the day of visit and through the selection process.
