TRAINING & PLACEMENT POLICY

INDIAN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY (INDIAN SCHOOL OF MINES) DHANBAD

For Company :

T&P cell will strictly follows the policy of allowing single candidate with single job/internship (i.e., One-Student-One-Job) irrespective of company status (private / PSU / Government / Education sector etc.) and CTC offered.

The company must complete their selection process in a single day.

The slot allotted by the placement office to the company is of 8 hours only. Although the companies are allowed to conduct test on the previous/same day to shortlist the candidates. The Start Time, End Time and Duration of Slot will be decided by the T&P Cell depending upon the number of companies pooled.

Interview process for any candidate must be completed by 8.00 PM on the same day and the result should be declared to T&P Cell only (not to students) by 9.00 PM on the same day. The T&P Cell will declare the results to the students before 11:00 PM.

No companies are allowed to interview a candidate for more than 45 minutes including Group Discussion and all rounds of interview (Technical + HR). Maximum 30 minutes will be provided to a company for all rounds of interview (Technical + HR) if their selection process does not have Group Discussion.

There is a high probability of multiple offers, the companies have to provide the waiting list at the end of the selection process so that when a candidate is selected in more than one company the wait listed candidate can be pushed for selection. The wait list will not be disclosed to the students and will be released on a per students basis.

The companies are required to be in touch with the SCPT and T&P Officials at all time. They should not entertain any direct contact from the students appearing in the placement process. In case of such situation, the company must bring this immediately to the notice of the placement office.

PSUs & Government organizations will be invited to conduct campus placement process any time during academic session. However, placement policy for PSUs & Government organizations are same that of others.

For Students :

Students (without any backlog) are only eligible for the placement / internship process.

T&P cell will strictly follow the policy of allowing single candidate with single job/internship (i.e., One-Student-One-Job) irrespective of company status (private / PSU / Government / Education sector etc.) and CTC offered.

The studentsâ€™ preference(for the companies sharing same day/slot) will be taken before the process and that will be used if he / she will be selected by more than one company. After his/her selection the candidate will not be allowed to sit for any other companies.

A student will not be entertained in placement process if he/she denied to take the job after his/her selection.

If the candidate appears in the PPI process during the Internship and he/she gets the PPO offer then the candidate will have to accept the offer and he/she will not be allowed to sit for placement process.

If the candidate register for a particular company then he/she has to appear for hiring the process of the respective company. In case he/she did not appear for the process then appropriate disciplinary action will be taken against the candidate.
