Academics & research IIT (ISM) reputed as a school of earth sciences from its inception has emerged into a one of the premium universities for technical education and research. IIT (ISM) has incorporated the tradition of academic excellence and innovation. The students who have qualified from the school have always brought laurels to the country and the school through their outstanding achievements and leadership qualities. Students get exposed to the highest quality of engineering education and research as well as an environment that instills in them a high potential to achieve any kind of objectives. The faculty of the institute have distinguished themselves through awards for academic activity from National as well as International organizations. A number of conferences, symposia and workshops are organized by the departments which attract participation from scholars all over the world. The School has fifteen academic departments and some specialized research centres that carry out the various academic activities. The academic curriculum requires the students to opt for various elective courses within their department. This gives them a broader perspective of the various research fields in their chosen domain. Co-curricular Activities Academic study at each level are balanced with a number of co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. The inventive and leadership spirit of the students is brought to the fore in the annual technocultural festival of IIT (ISM), Srijan, where the inventive engineering brains compete for success. Many departments also have their own technical festivals like Mechathlon & Sparx to foster the spirit of engineering. Apart from the Technical events IIT (ISM) also hosts various sporting events like Mukabula, Calcio Cup & Rebound which are National level Cricket, Football & Basketball Events respectively. There are also various active student societies like Cyber Society, Chaynika Society, Photographic Society, Cultural Scientific & Literary Society, Music Society, Sports Society, NSS , Yoga which promote the students in various extracurricular activities.
Programs Offered IIT (ISM) offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs leading to the award of B.Tech, Dual Degree,Integrated M.Tech, Integrated M.Sc.Tech, Integrated M.Sc., M.Sc, M.Sc.Tech, Ph.D and MBA degrees. Details of each of the programs and their admission processes are given below. B. Tech, Dual Degree & Integrated Courses M.Tech M.Sc. / M.Sc.Tech MBA Ph.D These programs are offered by various departments to the students who clear the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE), conducted by the IITs in April every year. World renowned for its ultra-competitive nature, the JEE attracts upto 300,000 candidates of which less than 5% secure an admission to the IIT's. B. Tech is a four year (eight semester) program, designed to impart the fundamentals of professional engineering. As per the academic curriculum, B.Tech students undertake a research project in the fourth year. Dual Degree is a combined B.Tech and M.Tech five year (ten semester) program, designed to give more emphasis to project work. The program is inline with B.Tech for three years. For the next two years, the students take up courses in their area of M.Tech specialization. In the fifth year, students do an exclusive M.Tech project. Integrated M.Tech is a five year (ten semester) program, admission into this program is given through JEE(Advanced). This program emphasizes on the industrial projects to address real-life issues and problems faced by the industries. By the end of the program students publish at least one research paper and are awarded a single post-graduate qualification. Integrated M.Sc / M.Sc.Tech is a five year (ten semester) program, admission into these programs is given to the candidates who cleared IIT-JEE
